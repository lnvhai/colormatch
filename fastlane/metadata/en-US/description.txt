Can you spot the odd color? Can you match the hue?

Color Match is a minimalist color perception game that combines two addictive challenges in one sleek dark app.

SPOT THE ODD COLOR
Scan the grid and tap the one tile that's different. Grids grow and color differences shrink as you advance — only sharp eyes survive.

MATCH THE COLOR
Study the target shade, adjust the sliders, and recreate it as closely as possible. One shot. Precision scored with perceptual color science.

• Two core modes — spot the difference and color matching
• Progressive difficulty that trains your eye over time
• Daily challenge with a fresh puzzle every day
• Haptic feedback and a clean, distraction-free dark UI
• Offline play — no account required
• Track your streak, best scores, and match precision

Color Match brings both mechanics together. Train your vision, beat your best, and see how dialed in your color sense really is.
